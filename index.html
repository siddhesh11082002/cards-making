MARVEL

Iron Man (Tony Stark)

Genius billionaire in a high-tech suit of armor, fighting threats with brains and tech.

Captain America (Steve Rogers)

A super-soldier from World War II, symbol of justice and freedom.

Spider-Man (Peter Parker)

Friendly neighborhood hero with spider powers and a heart of gold.

Thor Odinson

The Norse God of Thunder who defends Earth with his mighty hammer, Mjölnir.

Hulk (Bruce Banner)

A brilliant scientist who transforms into a powerful green rage monster.

Black Panther (T’Challa)

King of Wakanda, protector of his people, and master of Vibranium tech.

Doctor Strange (Stephen Strange)

Former surgeon turned Sorcerer Supreme who protects reality with magic.

Scarlet Witch (Wanda Maximoff)

Wields chaos magic and alters reality with immense power.

Ant-Man (Scott Lang)

A thief-turned-hero who can shrink and grow with advanced Pym tech.

Captain Marvel (Carol Danvers)

A cosmic-powered hero defending the galaxy with unmatched strength.

DC

Superman (Clark Kent / Kal-El)

The Man of Steel from Krypton, symbol of hope and strength.

Batman (Bruce Wayne)

Billionaire detective who uses fear, gadgets, and intellect to fight crime.

Wonder Woman (Diana Prince)

Amazonian warrior princess with divine powers and a lasso of truth.

Flash (Barry Allen)

The fastest man alive, using speed to battle evil across timelines.

Aquaman (Arthur Curry)

Half-human, half-Atlantean ruler of the seas who defends land and ocean alike.

Green Lantern (Hal Jordan)

Wields a power ring to create anything he imagines, fueled by willpower.

Cyborg (Victor Stone)

A human-machine hybrid with advanced technology and a heart of a hero.

Martian Manhunter (J'onn J'onzz)

A shape-shifting alien with telepathy, strength, and invisibility.

Shazam (Billy Batson)

A kid who transforms into a magical superhero by shouting "Shazam!"

Green Arrow (Oliver Queen)

Master archer and billionaire vigilante fighting corruption with precision.
